About Forest Fire Detection System

The Forest Fire Detection System is an innovative solution designed to detect and predict forest fires using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN).

Our system utilizes advanced image processing techniques to analyze aerial images and satellite data in real-time, allowing for early detection of potential fire outbreaks.

By leveraging CNN models trained on large datasets of forest fire images, our system can accurately identify fire-prone areas and provide timely alerts to authorities, enabling prompt response and mitigation efforts.

With the integration of machine learning algorithms and remote sensing technologies, our Forest Fire Detection System aims to mitigate the devastating impact of forest fires on the environment, wildlife, and human lives.
